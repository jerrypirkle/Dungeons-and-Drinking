The Bard

The bard's lute is out of tune and his poems are lewd. He introduces himself as Liam Longtongue, "Not a family name, mister. If you must know, ask your sister. And if she's too shy to answer true, well then you may ask your mother too." His fashionable clothes are colorful and well worn from travel. He offers to recite a poem for a small fee, or to insult you for free.

  • - If we banished all of the idiots from all of the villages of Chult and made them form their own village of idiots...you'd still be the village idiot.
  • - Not only are you short in stature, you mental midgets are fighting with back scratchers

  • - Unfortunately, you have caught my eye; And I have decided, that you ought to die
  • - He's not the first to have fallen dead; From the things that I've said
  • - Of words, I am king
    • Come at me, take a swing; And we'll see if your sword will sting; As much as these words that I sling;
  • - Well, I agree that your sword hurt more; But at least...my mother wasn't a whore
