Dungeons and Drinking

Tomb of Annihilation Adventure Log

Session 1

Our party has arrived in Port Nyanzaru
We are at the Thundering Lizard for some rest and some stew

We have been tasked by Sndra to find an artifact (soul monger)
But Chult is untamed and we first need a guide to put us on track

This town is rough, with unfamiliar tongue and rules
And is under constant assault, by roaming ghouls

Our adventure so far has already been hard
But at least we fared better than those poor town guards


Session 2

Ko won coin on the dino race
And Light made a deal to save some face

A bookie blackmailed him into a dirty deed
After he failed to steal the bokkie's gold

But in the end it was the bookie who was rolled
And the victim, now richer, was freed

We finally found our jungle guide
And set forth for gold, for glory, for pride

At the Righteous camp we did arrive
But we almost did not leave alive

We barely conqured the cursed gator temple
Because our party, well, we're sometimes somewhat simple

We then fought some goblin grunts who wanted our new jug
But we made short work of the runts and we made ready to chug


Session 3

We made our way to camp vengeance
Where their leader requested our attendance

He offered us lodging in his Fort
But in exchange, we were...compelled to escort his sick to port

In the end, the camp was little more than a pig pen
Full of bean-eating paladins: men who enjoyed the company of other men


Session 4

We met a group of tree lovers who tried turn us from our path
But rather than join their crusade, we kept our word headed back to town for a drink and a bath

We met with a kind merchant price
And our patron, so near death she was almost ash

But Queldar endured the stench and incense
And stuck our patron for more cash


Session 5

Just before we left port Nyanzaru
I ran into someone I once knew

A druid and botanist who used to sell me herb
And If I recall, his product was quite superd

A Goliath stronger, we once again entered the jungle
To find yet another adventure to bungle

Then we found a tower, a flaming finger
And while inside snack befriended a winged stranger

We then visited the bird's home in a cliff face
Where a whispering princess pleaded her case

And we were told of a magical rite
That would give us the gift of flight
